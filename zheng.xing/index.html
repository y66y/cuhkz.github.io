    Zheng Xing will receive his Ph.D. from School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, China in 2024. He received his Master's Degree from School of Electrical Science and Automation, Beihang University, Beijing, China, and Bachelor's degree from School of Electrical Science and the Automation, Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China. His research topic includes deep learning application in 5G Network and unsupervised learning.
